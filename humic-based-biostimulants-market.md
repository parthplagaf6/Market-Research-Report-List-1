<p><p>The humic-based biostimulants market comprises three main types: humic acid, fulvic acid, and potassium humate. Humic acid improves soil structure and enhances nutrient uptake, promoting robust plant growth. Fulvic acid, being smaller in molecular size, acts as a powerful chelator, allowing for better nutrient absorption and bioavailability. Potassium humate combines potassium with humic substances, boosting soil fertility and plant resilience. Together, these products support sustainable agriculture by improving soil health, enhancing crop productivity, and promoting environmental sustainability.</p></p>
