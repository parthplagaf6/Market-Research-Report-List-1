<p><p>The Machine Room Less (MRL) elevator market features two primary types: traction and hydraulic systems. </p><p>Traction MRL elevators utilize a system of pulleys and ropes, offering energy efficiency and higher speeds, making them ideal for mid to high-rise buildings. </p><p>In contrast, hydraulic MRL elevators rely on fluid pressure to raise and lower the cabin, providing more power for low-rise applications and smoother operations. Both types are engineered to save space by eliminating the need for a traditional machine room.</p></p>
