<p><p>Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) tape is categorized into four market types based on density: low density, standard density, medium density, and high density. Low density is flexible and suitable for applications requiring easy handling. Standard density offers a balance of strength and flexibility for general use. Medium density provides enhanced durability, ideal for demanding environments. High density is designed for extreme conditions, offering superior thickness and resistance, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications in industries such as plumbing and aerospace.</p></p>
