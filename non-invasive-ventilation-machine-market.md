<p><p>The non-invasive ventilation machine market primarily includes CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure) and BiPAP (Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure) devices. CPAP machines deliver a steady, continuous airflow to keep airways open, making them ideal for treating obstructive sleep apnea. Conversely, BiPAP machines provide varying pressures for inhalation and exhalation, benefiting patients with more complex respiratory issues or those requiring higher support levels. Both types enhance patient comfort and compliance, driving growth in the market as the demand for respiratory support continues to rise.</p></p>
