<p><strong>The Ink Cleaners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ink cleaners are categorized into two main market types: Type I and Type II. Type I ink cleaners are designed for quick and efficient removal of water-based inks, making them ideal for short runs or frequent color changes in printing processes. Type II ink cleaners, on the other hand, are formulated for deeper cleaning of solvent-based inks, suitable for longer production runs where stubborn residues are more prevalent. Both types serve critical roles in maintaining print quality and equipment longevity.</p></p>
