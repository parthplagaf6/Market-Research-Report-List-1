<p><p>Infrared imaging utilizes different infrared wavelengths for various applications in surveillance. Near Infrared (NIR) is used for short-range imaging in low light conditions. Short Wave Infrared (SWIR) excels in detecting moisture and certain materials, making it ideal for industrial applications. Mid Wave Infrared (MWIR) is effective for thermal imaging, detecting heat signatures from objects at medium ranges. Long Wave Infrared (LWIR) specializes in thermal imaging for longer distances, suitable for night surveillance and environmental monitoring, each fulfilling unique operational needs.</p></p>
