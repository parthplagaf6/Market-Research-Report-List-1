<p><p>The Daylight Fluorescent Pigments market is categorized into three main types: Thermoset, Thermoplastic, and Other variations such as Aqueous Dispersions. Thermoset pigments are durable and resistant to heat, making them ideal for applications requiring permanence. Thermoplastic pigments, on the other hand, can be remelted and reshaped, offering flexibility in production. Other types, like aqueous dispersions, provide environmentally friendly options for various applications, promoting stability and ease of use in different mediums. Each type serves specific industry needs, enhancing product visibility and aesthetics.</p></p>
