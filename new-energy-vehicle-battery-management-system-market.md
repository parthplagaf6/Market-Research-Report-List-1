<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle (NEV) Battery Management System (BMS) market is categorized into three types: distributed, centralized, and modular. </p><p>A distributed BMS features multiple control units spread across the battery pack, allowing for better fault tolerance and real-time monitoring. Centralized BMS utilizes a single control unit for the entire battery system, simplifying management but potentially increasing risk if that unit fails. Modular BMS combines aspects of both, using multiple interconnected modules for flexibility and scalability while ensuring robust monitoring and management capabilities.</p></p>
