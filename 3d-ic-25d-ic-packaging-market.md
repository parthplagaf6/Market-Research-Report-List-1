<p><p>3D IC and 2.5D IC packaging are advanced semiconductor packaging technologies that enhance the performance and efficiency of integrated circuits. 3D IC packaging refers to stacking multiple layers of ICs vertically, allowing for shorter interconnects, reduced power consumption, and increased functionality within a compact footprint. Emerging applications in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and high-performance computing further drive the need for these advanced packaging solutions. </p><p>Innovative advancements, such as fan-out wafer-level packaging and through-silicon vias, are enhancing the functionality and efficiency of IC packages. Together, these technologies enable more efficient use of space and improved electrical performance, driving advancements in electronic device capabilities.</p></p>
