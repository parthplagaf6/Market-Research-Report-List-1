<p><p>The liquid culture media market is characterized by two primary types: serum-free media and serum media. Serum-free media is designed to support cell growth without the use of animal serum, reducing variability and improving consistency, making it suitable for sensitive applications such as biopharmaceutical production. In contrast, serum media contains animal serum, providing essential growth factors and nutrients, and is widely used for general cell culture applications. Both types serve critical roles in research, biotechnology, and pharmaceutical industries.</p></p>
