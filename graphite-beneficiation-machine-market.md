<p><strong>The Graphite Beneficiation Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flotation</li><li>Electrical Seperation</li><li>Re-election</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The graphite beneficiation machine market consists of three primary types: flotation, electrical separation, and re-election. Flotation involves using chemical reagents to separate graphite from impurities based on hydrophobic properties. Electrical separation utilizes differences in conductivity to isolate graphite from other minerals. Re-election, or gravity separation, focuses on exploiting density differences to preferentially concentrate graphite. Each method plays a crucial role in optimizing the extraction process, enhancing the purity, and improving the overall efficiency of graphite production.</p></p>
