<p><p>The card reader-writer market encompasses several types, each serving different functions. Contact card reader-writers require physical contact with the card's chip for data transfer, commonly used in secure environments. Contactless card reader-writers utilize RFID technology for quick transactions without physical contact, ideal for fast-paced settings. Biometric card reader-writers enhance security by incorporating fingerprint or facial recognition, ensuring user authentication. Magnetic-stripe card reader-writers read data from magnetic strips found on cards, widely used in retail and financial transactions. Each type addresses specific security and convenience needs.</p></p>
