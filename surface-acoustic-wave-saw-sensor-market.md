<p><ul><li>Resonators</li><li>Delay Lines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) sensor market encompasses two primary types: resonators and delay lines. Resonators utilize the resonance of acoustic waves on a piezoelectric substrate to detect changes in frequency, making them suitable for sensing applications like temperature and pressure. Delay lines, on the other hand, measure time delays of acoustic waves traveling across a medium, allowing for high-precision sensing in applications such as liquid level detection and gas monitoring. Both types leverage the unique properties of surface acoustic waves for various industrial applications.</p></p>
