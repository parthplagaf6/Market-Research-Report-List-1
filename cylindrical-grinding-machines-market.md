<p><p>Cylindrical grinding machines are classified into three main types: CNC, manually-controlled, and PLC-controlled. CNC machines utilize computer numerical control for precision and automation, enabling complex shapes and high repeatability. Manually-controlled machines require skilled operators to adjust settings and perform grinding tasks, offering flexibility but less consistency. PLC-controlled machines use programmable logic controllers for semi-automation, combining some manual inputs with automated processes to balance precision and operator control, catering to various manufacturing needs and production volumes.</p></p>
