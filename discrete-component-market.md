<p><p>The discrete component market includes various semiconductor devices essential for electronic circuits. Diodes allow current to flow in one direction, providing rectification and voltage regulation. MOSFETs (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistors) are crucial for switching and amplification in digital circuits. IGBTs (Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistors) combine the characteristics of MOSFETs and BJTs (Bipolar Junction Transistors), making them ideal for high-power applications. Other components in this market may include transistors, rectifiers, and specialty devices, each serving specific functions in electronic systems.</p></p>
