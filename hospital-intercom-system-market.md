<p><p>The hospital intercom system market is primarily divided into two types: wired and wireless systems. Wired systems rely on physical cables for connectivity, offering reliable communication with minimal interference, but their installation can be more complex and costly. Conversely, wireless systems utilize radio frequencies to facilitate communication, providing flexibility and ease of installation, particularly in retrofitting existing facilities. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing patient care and operational efficiency in healthcare environments, catering to different hospital needs and infrastructure capabilities.</p></p>
