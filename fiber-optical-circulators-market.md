<p><p>Fiber optical circulators are specialized devices used in optical networks to control the direction of light signals. The market is segmented into two main types: 3-port and 4-port circulators. </p><p>3-port circulators allow signals to be redirected from one port to the next in a cyclical manner, making them ideal for applications like laser systems and fiber amplifiers. In contrast, 4-port circulators provide more flexibility by enabling multiple input and output paths, enhancing network efficiency for complex communication systems.</p></p>
