<p><strong>The PUR Insulation Foams Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Open-Cell Foam"</li><li>"Closed-Cell Foam"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PUR insulation foams market is primarily divided into two types: open-cell foam and closed-cell foam. Open-cell foam features a flexible structure with interconnected cells, allowing for excellent sound absorption and thermal insulation. It is lightweight and commonly used in interior applications. In contrast, closed-cell foam has a denser composition with sealed cells, providing superior moisture resistance and structural support. This type is ideal for exterior applications and energy-efficient insulation solutions, offering higher durability and insulation value.</p></p>
