<p><ul><li>Carboxylic Acid Type Polyurethane</li><li>Sulfonic Acid Type Polyurethane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anionic aqueous polyurethane market includes two primary types: carboxylic acid type and sulfonic acid type polyurethanes. Carboxylic acid type polyurethanes are characterized by the incorporation of carboxyl groups, enhancing their water dispersibility and adhesion properties, making them suitable for coatings and adhesives. In contrast, sulfonic acid type polyurethanes contain sulfonic acid functionalities, offering improved stability and performance in low pH environments. Both types are widely used in industries such as textiles, automotive, and construction, due to their eco-friendly characteristics.</p></p>
