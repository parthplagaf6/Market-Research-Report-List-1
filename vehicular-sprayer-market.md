In contrast, motor-driven sprayers rely on electric motors, offering quieter operation and lower emissions, making them suitable for urban settings and smaller agricultural tasks. Both types cater to diverse needs in pest control, crop management, and land maintenance, enhancing efficiency in spraying operations.</p></p>
