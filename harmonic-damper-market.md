<p><p>The harmonic damper market includes various types: elastomer dampers, which use rubber-like materials to absorb vibrations; viscous dampers, which employ fluid to dissipate energy through internal friction; and friction-style dampers, which rely on friction between surfaces to mitigate oscillations.
