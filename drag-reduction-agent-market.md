<p><p>The Drag Reduction Agent (DRA) market is categorized into two main types: powder and emulsion. Powder-type DRAs are solid formulations that are typically used in dry or low-moisture environments, offering ease of transport and storage. Emulsion-type DRAs, on the other hand, are liquid formulations that provide better dispersion in fluids, making them suitable for aqueous applications. Both types enhance the flow of fluids in pipelines by reducing friction, thereby improving efficiency in oil and gas transportation.</p></p>
