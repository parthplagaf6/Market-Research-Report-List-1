<p><p>The mechanical lithotriptor market is categorized into single-use and reusable types. Single-use lithotriptors are designed for one-time applications, enhancing patient safety by minimizing infection risks and ensuring optimal performance. In contrast, reusable lithotriptors offer cost efficiency and environmental benefits, as they can be sterilized and utilized multiple times. Both types cater to different healthcare settings, with single-use devices often preferred in outpatient procedures, while reusable ones are favored in hospitals where high-volume usage is expected.</p></p>
