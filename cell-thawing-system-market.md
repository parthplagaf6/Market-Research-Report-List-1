<p><ul><li>Water-Free Cell Thawing System</li><li>Water Bath Cell Thawing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cell Thawing System market includes two primary types: Water-Free Cell Thawing Systems and Water Bath Cell Thawing Systems. Water-Free Systems utilize alternative methods, such as air-based thawing or specialized thawing containers, to quickly and efficiently thaw cells without the need for water, reducing contamination risks. In contrast, Water Bath Systems use temperature-controlled water baths to uniformly thaw frozen cell samples. Each system offers distinct advantages in terms of efficiency, ease of use, and safety for cell preservation and research applications.</p></p>
