<p><p>Detonation flame arresters are crucial safety devices designed to prevent the propagation of flames in hazardous environments. The market for these arresters is categorized into two main types: in-line and end-of-line. In-line arresters are installed within pipelines, allowing for continuous flow while protecting against flames traveling through the piping system. End-of-line arresters, on the other hand, are positioned at the terminal of a pipeline, safeguarding the system from backflow flames. Both types are essential for explosion protection in various industrial applications.</p></p>
