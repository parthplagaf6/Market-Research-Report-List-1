<p><p>The skin wound dressings market is categorized into several types, including passive and bioactive dressings. Passive dressings primarily serve a mechanical function, providing a protective barrier against external contaminants while maintaining a moist environment to promote healing. In contrast, bioactive dressings are designed to actively enhance the healing process by incorporating therapeutic agents like antimicrobials or growth factors. These advanced dressings not only protect the wound site but also stimulate cellular activity, facilitating faster and more effective healing.</p></p>
