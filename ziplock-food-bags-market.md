<p><p>Ziplock food bags are categorized by the materials used in their production, which impact their durability, flexibility, and barrier properties. Polyethylene (PE) is the most common material, known for its cost-effectiveness and moisture resistance. Polyvinyl chloride (PVC) offers enhanced clarity and strength but can be less flexible. Polyvinylidene chloride (PVDC) provides superior gas barrier properties, making it ideal for preserving food quality. 'Others' may include emerging materials and biodegradable options, catering to environmentally conscious consumers.</p></p>
