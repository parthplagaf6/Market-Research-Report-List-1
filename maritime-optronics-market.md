<p><ul><li>Multispectral</li><li>Hyperspectral</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Maritime Optronics market is categorized into multispectral and hyperspectral segments. Multispectral systems capture data across specific wavelengths, typically visible to infrared, enabling efficient detection and identification of objects in various marine environments. Hyperspectral systems, on the other hand, gather data across a broader spectrum, providing more detailed information by analyzing numerous narrow bands. These technologies enhance maritime surveillance, reconnaissance, and environmental monitoring, allowing for improved decision-making and operational effectiveness in naval and commercial applications.</p></p>
